GOMOKU GAME RULES
=================

HOW TO PLAY:
- Players take turns placing one stone on an empty intersection
- First to get 5 stones in a row wins (horizontal, vertical or diagonal)
- The five stones must be unbroken with no gaps

VALID MOVES:
- Must be placed on an empty cell
- Cannot jump, replace or remove stones

STRATEGY TIPS:
1) Create multiple potential winning lines at once
2) Block opponent's 4-in-a-row threats
3) Plan ahead - the game rewards foresight
